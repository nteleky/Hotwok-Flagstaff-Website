Karaoke

Hot Wok is happy to accommodate large parties for special occasions and get-togethers. As part of our endeavor for an authentic Chinese experience at Hot Wok, we have an on-premise private karaoke room designed in the "KTV" style popular in Asia. Along with a temperature-controlled room, a state-of-the-art sound system, beverage services and a comfortable group setting, guests will receive access to an almost unlimited archive of karaoke music, from old time classics to contemporary hits. To inquire about rates and availability, please call us at 928-226-1007 or come visit us at our location on Milton.
